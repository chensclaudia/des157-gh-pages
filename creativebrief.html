Character Brief

I enjoyed creating these data visualizations. I wanted to create the most complete picture of what it was like to work at IBM as possible. For some pieces of data (like the first chart) I couldn't provide an explanation for why the data was the way it is, but for others I could provide more insight. Of course this is very far to how IBM workers are actually like, (how well can we all be represented in parcels of information?), but it is still interesting to see this data and make our own observations.
