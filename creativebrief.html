Creative Brief

Background/Overview

One of the most impactful aspects of our lives is food. It brings communities together and brings us a great deal of happiness. Although it seems many of us have our love of food in common, it’s still only shared amongst tight-groups. We all dread the awkward first date dinners especially compared to a night out with a group of friends we haven’t seen in a while. There’s a ton of dishes we all have yet to explore, thus missing out on. It’s also not readily available to everyone when they want it/need it. We aim to change these things.

Objective

To bring together communities and enrich each others’ lives with different accessible dishes.

Target Audience

University students, specifically UCD students. Ones who are eager to explore and engage with their peers through food. Students in circumstances where food isn’t easily accessible or students who are willing to share new dishes/leftovers.

Big Idea

Bringing all kinds of people together with all kinds of food.

Impact

We have all hit rough patches in our lives. From rationing ramen everyday to studying so late into the night buying or making food is just too exhausting. Or we’re just tired of eating the same thing everyday but we don’t have the knowledge to make anything better. We can enrich each other’s lives by sharing and introducing to each other new food.
