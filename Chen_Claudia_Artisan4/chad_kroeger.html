CHAD KROEGER

Chad Kroeger was born Chad Robert Turton on November 15th 1974 in Hanna, Alberta, Canada. Kroeger learned guitar at the age of 13 and began his grunge-cover band in the early 1990s. Following the band's separation, Kroeger formed Nickelback alongside his older half-brother, Mike and Ryan Peake, with Kroeger as the lead guitarist. Their success moved them from Hanna to Vancouver and eventually into mainstream U.S. media.

Kroeger's lyrics consist of motifs such as heartbreak, fatherhood, sex, drugs, and alcoholism. Nickelback's music has been featured in hit films like Charlie's Angels and Spider-Man. They have collaborated with other successful artists such as Creed, 3 Doors Down and Bo Bice.

In July 2012 Kroeger began dating fellow Canadian musician Avril Lavigne after collaborating on her self-titled album together. The following August they were engaged. In September 2015, however, they announced their separation.
